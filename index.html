Got Questions?

We've got answers

Does it work on carpet?

No — the ED12 Lite is built for hard floors: tile, hardwood, laminate, and vinyl (LVP). It's not designed for carpet use.

How long does the battery actually last?

About 30 minutes in Normal mode, 25 in Max mode. That's enough for 2–3 rooms or up to 100 m². Then dock and charge.

Will pet hair wrap around the brush?

No. The dual comb scrapers actively cut hair before it can tangle. In independent tests: zero hair stuck on the roller after a full session.

How loud is it?

Maximum 76 dBA — quieter than most traditional vacuums. Roughly the same as a normal conversation.

What comes in the box?

The ED12 Lite unit, charging base, 2× mop rollers, 2× filters, hair cleaning tool, and a bottle of cleaning solution.

Can I use regular tap water?

Yes, tap water works perfectly. You can also add a cleaning tablet for a fresher scent — totally optional.
